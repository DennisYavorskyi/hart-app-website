Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 22, 2026

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Denys Yavorskyi ("I", "me", or "my") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use HART: Habit & Routine Tracker ("the App"). By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information as described in this policy.

2. Information I Collect

Information you provide:

  • Email address and password (for account creation)
  • Display name
  • Profile avatar (optional, Pro users only)
  • Habit names, descriptions, and completion data you enter

Information collected automatically:

  • Device type and operating system (via Expo/RevenueCat)
  • Purchase and subscription status (via RevenueCat)
  • Push notification token (only if you enable notifications)
  • Advertising identifier (only for free tier users, via Google AdMob)

3. How I Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your account
  • To sync your habits across devices (Pro users)
  • To process and manage your subscription via RevenueCat
  • To send push notifications (only if you enable them)
  • To show relevant ads to free tier users via Google AdMob
  • To provide customer support

4. Data Storage

  • Free tier data is stored locally on your device using SQLite
  • Pro tier data is synced to secure cloud servers via Supabase, hosted in data centers that comply with industry-standard security practices
  • Payment processing is handled entirely by RevenueCat and Apple/Google — I never see or store your payment information

5. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services, each governed by their own privacy policies:

  • Supabase (supabase.com) — database and authentication
  • RevenueCat (revenuecat.com) — subscription management
  • Expo (expo.dev) — app infrastructure and push notifications
  • Google AdMob (admob.google.com) — advertising for free tier users
  • Apple App Store / Google Play — payment processing

6. Advertising

Free tier users will see banner advertisements served by Google AdMob. These ads may use your device's advertising identifier to show relevant ads. You can limit ad tracking in your device settings:

  • iOS: Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking
  • Android: Settings → Google → Ads → Delete advertising ID

Pro users do not see any advertisements.

7. Data Sharing

I do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. I only share data with the third-party services listed above, strictly to operate the App.

8. Data Retention

  • Your data is retained as long as your account is active
  • You can delete your account and all associated data at any time by contacting deniusproductions@gmail.com
  • Local device data can be deleted by uninstalling the App

9. Children's Privacy

HART is rated 4+ and is suitable for all ages. I do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent. If you believe a child has provided personal information without consent, please contact me at deniusproductions@gmail.com.

10. Your Rights (Canadian Users — PIPEDA)

Under Canada's Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act (PIPEDA), you have the right to:

  • Access the personal information I hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Withdraw consent for data collection by deleting your account
  • File a complaint with the Office of the Privacy Commissioner of Canada

11. Security

I take reasonable steps to protect your information using industry-standard security practices including encrypted connections (HTTPS), secure authentication via Supabase, and no storage of payment data.

12. Changes to This Policy

I may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. I will notify you of significant changes via the App or email. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance.
